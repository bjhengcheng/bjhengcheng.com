Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2023

Beijing Hengcheng Property Management Co., Ltd ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information across our mobile applications and services, in compliance with global standards including Google Play Developer Policies, Apple App Store Review Guidelines, GDPR, and CCPA.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information that you provide directly to us or through your use of our applications, including:

  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, unique device identifiers (e.g., IDFA, GAID).
  • Usage Data: Interaction with app features, session duration, and in-app purchases.
  • Personal Data: Email addresses or social media profiles if provided during account registration.

2. Third-Party Platforms & Monetization

Our applications may integrate with third-party services for monetization and analytics. We comply with the policies of:

  • App Stores: Google Play Store and Apple App Store.
  • Ad Networks: AdMob, Unity Ads, AppLovin, and IronSource.
  • Analytics: Firebase Analytics, Google Analytics.

These third parties may collect data to provide personalized advertisements and analyze traffic. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies.

3. Global Privacy Compliance

We adhere to international data protection regulations:

  • GDPR (Europe): We ensure a legal basis for processing data of EU residents and provide rights to access, rectify, or erase personal data.
  • CCPA (California): We provide California residents with the right to know what personal information is collected and the right to opt-out of the sale of personal information.

4. Age Management & COPPA

We prioritize the safety of children. Our apps comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or 16 in certain jurisdictions) without parental consent. If we become aware of such data collection, we will take immediate steps to delete it.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@bjhengcheng.com.
